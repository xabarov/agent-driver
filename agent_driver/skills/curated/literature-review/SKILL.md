# Literature Review

Group sources by method, finding, and limitation rather than summarizing one
paper at a time. Prefer primary papers, proceedings, datasets, and official
project pages. Track what each source actually supports and avoid filling gaps
with search snippets.

Finish with themes, disagreements, and open questions.
